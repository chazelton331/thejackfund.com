ABOUT THE JACK FUND

Facilitating adventure, exploration, and celebration!

The Jack Fund was established to honor the life of Sean Collins by providing living legacy opportunities to his son Jack. Sean was a man of adventure, exploration and celebration. This fund intends to share those ideals and the memory of Sean by providing Jack opportunities to participate in activities that best reflect the spirit of his father.

These opportunities will be focused towards social and leisure activities that Jack can participate in and will be paid for by money collected for the fund. The Fund Committee will work to determine the most appropriate opportunities. All projects will need to be met with final approval of Wendy Collins prior to Jack participating.
